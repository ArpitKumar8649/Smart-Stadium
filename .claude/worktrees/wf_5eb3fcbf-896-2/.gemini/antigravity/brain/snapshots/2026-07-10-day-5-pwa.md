### D5.1 / D5.2 — Progressive Web App Configuration
- Installed and configured `vite-plugin-pwa`.
- Generated `manifest.webmanifest` and `sw.js` (Service Worker) automatically.
- Defined caching strategies:
  - **Static assets & geometry (`floor.geojson`)**: Cached aggressively for immediate offline load.

  - **Navigation Routes**: Cached for 24 hours so frequent paths do not require re-fetching.

- The banner displays immediately across the app globally when a network drop is detected, providing transparent feedback that cached data is being displayed.
